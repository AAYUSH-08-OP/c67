Once upon a time in a forest, there lived a monkey who resides on a berry tree,
which was on the banks of the river.In the same forest,there lived a crocodile and his wife.
One day the crocodile come to the banks of the river and rested under a tree.
The kind hearted monkey offered him some fruits.The crocodile came back the next day for some more fruits
as he loved them.And the days passed by the crocodile and monkey became good friends.
